# Rank and Rent Compliance Guidelines

## Core Rules

### Never Claim Past Projects or Specific Clients
- Frame scenarios as examples of the type of engagement we can handle
- Invite contact to share real references upon request
- Do not fabricate timelines or outcomes

### No Testimonials or Unverifiable Claims
- No testimonials, no review stars, no badges without sources
- No unverifiable claims about past performance
- No specific client names or project details

### Tax and Legal Disclaimers
- Educational content only, not tax, legal, or investment advice
- 1031 defers income tax on qualifying real property and does not remove transfer or documentary taxes
- Users should consult a Qualified Intermediary and tax advisor before acting

### Securities Disclaimers
- If DST or TIC is mentioned, include a securities disclaimer
- We do not sell securities
- We provide introductions to licensed providers only

### Footer Disclosure
This site routes inquiries to our chosen fulfillment partner for 1031 exchange advisory and property identification support.

## Content Standards

### Language
- Plain English
- No contractions
- No em dashes
- Short sentences
- Technical accuracy

### Location References
- Include city and state in required places
- Do not make up information about locations
- Verify all location-specific claims

### Property Information
- Do not fabricate property details
- Use verified sources only
- Clearly indicate when information is estimated or example
